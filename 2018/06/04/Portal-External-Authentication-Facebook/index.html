Portal External Authentication - Facebook

Posted on June 4, 2018 本文总阅读量次

In CRM Portal, you are able to register it with your personal email address, then you are able to sign in the Portal internally. Actually, you don’t need to register Portal with email address if the Portal enable other providers for external authentication, eg: Facebook, Google, etc. Afterwards, you can sign in the Portal with these external accounts.

Here I will introduce how to enable these external authentication in CRM Portal. Facebook first.

  1. Log on your Facebook and go to https://developers.facebook.com to create an new App. Fill out the “Display Name” and “Contact Email” and Create App ID.

  2. After click “Create App ID”, you may receive a “Security Check” dialog, type the code.

  3. In the following “Add a Product” page, select “Facebook Login” tile as we will use Facebook account to log on Portal.

  4. Afterwards, click Settings -> Basic on the left panel, then fill out the “Privacy Policy URL” and select one “category”.

    On the bottom, click “Add Platform”, choose the “Website” and type your Portal URL in it. Then Save changes.

    Make a note of the “App ID” and “App Secret”, we will use them later.

    PS: For the “Privacy Policy URL”, we can google it and apply some free Private Policy, eg: from https://termsfeed.com/ or https://www.freeprivacypolicy.com

    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BIrC8ke_JWw&feature=youtu.be

  5. Navigate to Facebook Login -> Settings, fill out the “Valid OAuth Redirect URLs” as https:///signin-facebook. Then Save the changes.

  6. Then go to App Reviews, make this app public.

  7. Go to the Portal associated CRM organization, navigate to Portals -> Site Settings, fill out the Site Settings “Authentication/OpenAuth/Facebook/AppId“ and “Authentication/OpenAuth/Facebook/AppSecret“ with the “App ID” and “App Secret” we have noted before.

    1. Go to the Portal sign in page, we can see the “Facebook” button appears. Now you can sign in with your Facebook account. Cheers!!
